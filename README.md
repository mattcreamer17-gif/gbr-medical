# GBR Medical landing page

One-page bespoke sales site prepared for GBR Medical ahead of eMerge Americas 2026 (booth 323). The page lifts the GBR Medical brand tokens directly from their live Astra CSS variables: single medical blue (#0170B9), system-font stack, generous white space with light-grey alternating section breaks, and their own horizontal wordmark logo at the top. Copy is the fit-analysis pitch body reflowed into a hero, a pain read, the 60-30-10 Eduba frame with a small stat row, the Feeld sprint case study (value-based language, no pricing), the Ethics Engine reference with required bio and hyperlinks, and a single CTA to thirty minutes with Matt Creamer. Eduba branding appears only in the footer. Mobile responsive at 375px.

Intended URL path: /for/gbr-medical
